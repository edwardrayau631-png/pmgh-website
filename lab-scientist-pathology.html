Position: Laboratory Scientist – Pathology

Department: Pathology Services

Location: PMGH, Korobosea

Type: Full time

About the role

As a Laboratory Scientist, you will perform diagnostic tests across haematology, microbiology and biochemistry to support accurate diagnosis and treatment of patients at PMGH.

Key responsibilities

  • Perform high-quality laboratory testing across core pathology disciplines.
  • Ensure proper sample collection, handling and storage.
  • Maintain laboratory equipment and quality control standards.
  • Record, analyse and verify test results.
  • Work collaboratively with clinicians and nursing staff.

Essential requirements

  • Degree in Medical Laboratory Science or equivalent.
  • Registration with relevant PNG regulatory bodies.
  • Experience in diagnostic laboratory settings.
  • Strong attention to detail and safety protocols.

Desirable

  • Experience in a hospital or high-volume laboratory.
  • Knowledge of laboratory information systems.
