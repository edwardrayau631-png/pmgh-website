Position: Laboratory Scientist – Pathology

Department: Pathology Services

Location: Port Moresby General Hospital, Korobosea

Employment type: Full time

About the role

The Laboratory Scientist plays a key role in performing diagnostic tests across haematology, microbiology, biochemistry and other laboratory disciplines. The role supports timely and accurate diagnosis for PMGH patients.

Key responsibilities

  • Conduct diagnostic laboratory tests with accuracy and efficiency.
  • Ensure proper sample collection, handling and storage procedures.
  • Operate and maintain laboratory equipment.
  • Perform quality assurance checks and follow safety protocols.
  • Collaborate with clinicians, nurses and other health professionals.

Essential requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Medical Laboratory Science or equivalent.
  • Registration with the appropriate PNG regulatory authority.
  • Knowledge of laboratory procedures, safety and quality standards.
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.

Desirable

  • Experience working in a busy hospital laboratory environment.
  • Familiarity with digital laboratory information systems.
