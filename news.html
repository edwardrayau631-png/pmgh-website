Latest News & Announcements

Stay updated with current projects, improvements, clinical developments and community programs at Port Moresby General Hospital. PMGH continues to expand services, strengthen infrastructure, and support medical training across Papua New Guinea.

ICU and Mortuary Redevelopment Project

Project Update · 2025

PMGH is progressing the redevelopment of the Intensive Care Unit and mortuary facilities. The upgrade will expand capacity, improve infection control, and create a more dignified and comfortable environment for patients and families. Construction works are ongoing.

Expansion of Teaching & Training Programs

Education & Training · 2025

As PNG’s national teaching hospital, PMGH continues to strengthen collaboration with the University of Papua New Guinea's School of Medicine & Health Sciences. New clinical placement opportunities and postgraduate pathways are being supported across departments.

Upgrades to Patient & Visitor Facilities

Facilities · 2024

Improvements to wards, outpatient areas, public seating, and access routes are underway. These upgrades aim to enhance comfort, safety, and accessibility for patients and visitors.

Support for Community Blood Donation Initiatives

Community · 2024

PMGH continues to participate in and support community blood drives. The blood bank encourages groups, businesses and organisations to take part in scheduled donation activities to support life-saving transfusion needs.

Infection Prevention & Mask Guidance

Public Health · 2024

Visitors are encouraged to follow current infection control guidance. Masks may be recommended in some clinical areas to protect immunocompromised patients.
