ICU and Mortuary Redevelopment Project

Project update · 2025

Construction progress continues on the redevelopment of PMGH’s Intensive Care Unit and Mortuary facilities. The upgrade will expand bed capacity, improve infection control measures, and create a safer, more dignified space for patients and families.

Strengthening Teaching & Training Programs

Education & training · 2025

PMGH continues to expand its teaching and clinical training partnerships with the University of Papua New Guinea. New placements and postgraduate development pathways are being added to support the next generation of healthcare professionals.

Improved Patient & Visitor Facilities

Facilities · 2024

Ongoing improvements across wards and public spaces are enhancing patient experience, visitor comfort, and accessibility. Upgrades include seating, shade, wayfinding signage, and refurbishment of key clinical areas.

Community Blood Donation Initiatives

Community · 2024

PMGH continues to work with communities and organisations to encourage voluntary blood donation. Donors play a critical role in supporting surgery, trauma care and emergency services.
