ICU and mortuary redevelopment project

Project update · 2025

Construction and planning are underway to redevelop the intensive care unit and mortuary at PMGH. The project aims to increase capacity, improve infection control and provide a more dignified environment for patients and families.

Expanding teaching and training opportunities

Education & training · 2025

As the national teaching hospital, PMGH continues to expand clinical placements and specialist training for medical, nursing and allied health students in partnership with UPNG.

Improving patient and visitor facilities

Facilities · 2024

The hospital is progressively upgrading wards and public areas to improve access, comfort and safety for patients, carers and visitors.

Community blood donation initiatives

Community · 2024

PMGH supports blood donation drives and encourages communities, businesses and organisations to take part in voluntary blood donation to save lives.
