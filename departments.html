PMGH has a wide range of clinical, diagnostic and support departments that work together to provide safe and high-quality care for patients across Papua New Guinea.

Emergency Department

24/7 acute care for trauma, accidents and critical conditions.

Medical Services

General medicine, infectious diseases, cardiology & more.

Surgical Services

Operating theatres, surgical wards and postoperative care.

Maternity & Obstetrics

Maternal health, childbirth services and antenatal clinics.

Paediatrics

Medical and surgical care for infants, children and adolescents.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Critical care for life-threatening conditions.

Radiology & Imaging

X-ray, CT scan, ultrasound and diagnostic imaging.

Laboratory Services

Blood tests, pathology and diagnostic laboratory support.

Pharmacy

Medication dispensing and clinical pharmacy support.
