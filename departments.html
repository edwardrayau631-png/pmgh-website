PMGH is organised into clinical, diagnostics, emergency and support departments that work together to provide comprehensive care for patients across Papua New Guinea.

Clinical departments

Emergency Department

24-hour emergency care for critical and urgent conditions.

Medical Ward

General medicine and specialty units including cardiology, nephrology and more.

Surgical Ward

General surgery, orthopaedics, ENT, obstetrics and gynaecology.

Paediatrics

Specialised care for infants, children and adolescents.

Maternity & Obstetrics

Antenatal, labour ward, delivery suites and postnatal services.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Critical care for severely ill patients requiring monitoring and ventilation.

Diagnostics & support units

Radiology & Imaging

X-ray, ultrasound and CT imaging services.

Pathology Laboratory

Microbiology, biochemistry, haematology and diagnostic testing.

Pharmacy

Medication dispensing, counselling and clinical pharmacy services.

Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation, mobility assistance and post-operative therapy.

Family Support Centre

Medical and psychosocial support for survivors of violence.

Education & Training

Clinical teaching, student placements and workforce development.
