PMGH is organised into a range of clinical departments and specialist units. Below are some of the key areas involved in providing care.

Emergency Department

24/7 emergency and acute care for serious illness, trauma and accidents.

Medical Services

General medicine, cardiology, infectious diseases, respiratory and other subspecialties.

Surgical Services

Operating theatres, surgical wards and peri-operative care for a range of procedures.

Maternity & Obstetrics

Labour ward, antenatal and postnatal services for mothers and babies.

Paediatrics

Medical and surgical care for infants, children and young people.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Critical care for patients requiring close monitoring and advanced life support.

Radiology & Imaging

X-ray, ultrasound and other imaging services supporting diagnosis and treatment.

Pathology & Laboratory

Laboratory testing including haematology, microbiology and biochemistry.

Pharmacy

Medication supply, clinical pharmacy services and advice for patients and staff.

Family Support Centre

Specialist service providing care and support to survivors of family and sexual violence.

Allied Health & Support

Including physiotherapy, social work and other support services.

Education & Training

Clinical teaching, supervision and continuing professional development for staff and students.
