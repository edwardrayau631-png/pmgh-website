PMGH is organised into a range of clinical departments and specialist units that work together to provide comprehensive care.

Emergency Department

24/7 emergency and acute care for serious illness, trauma and accidents.

Medical Services

General medicine and medical subspecialties such as cardiology and infectious diseases.

Surgical Services

Operating theatres and surgical wards providing peri-operative and post-operative care.

Maternity & Obstetrics

Labour ward, antenatal, intrapartum and postnatal care for mothers and babies.

Paediatrics

Inpatient and outpatient care for infants, children and young people.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Critical care for patients requiring close monitoring and life support.

Radiology & Imaging

X-ray, ultrasound and other imaging services supporting diagnosis and treatment.

Pathology & Laboratory

Laboratory testing including haematology, microbiology and biochemistry.

Pharmacy

Medication supply, clinical pharmacy services and advice for patients and staff.

Family Support Centre

Specialist support for survivors of family and sexual violence.

Allied Health & Support

Physiotherapy, social work and other services supporting patient recovery.

Education & Training

Clinical teaching, supervision and continuing professional development.
