Position: Security Officer – Hospital Services

Department: Corporate & Support Services

Location: PMGH, Korobosea

Type: Full time

About the role

As a Security Officer at PMGH, you will help maintain a safe and secure hospital environment, assisting patients, visitors and staff across all wards and public areas.

Key responsibilities

  • Monitor hospital premises and prevent security incidents.
  • Assist patients and visitors with directions and access.
  • Support staff in high-risk situations when required.
  • Respond to alarms, disturbances or emergencies.
  • Maintain accurate security logs and reports.

Essential requirements

  • Experience in security or protective services.
  • Good communication and conflict-resolution skills.
  • Ability to work shifts (day and night).
  • Physically fit and reliable.

Desirable

  • Experience in hospital or large public-service environments.
  • Basic first-aid knowledge.
