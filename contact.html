Visiting information

Visiting hours and access rules may change depending on public health requirements and clinical conditions. Please call the main switchboard for the latest information before visiting.

  • Check with the ward before visiting a patient.
  • Wash or sanitise hands when entering and leaving clinical areas.
  • Masks may be recommended in high-risk or specialised units.
  • Do not visit if you are unwell or have respiratory symptoms.
