About Port Moresby General Hospital

Papua New Guinea’s national referral and teaching hospital.

Home › About

Our role

Port Moresby General Hospital (PMGH) is PNG’s largest public hospital and the nation’s flagship tertiary, referral and teaching facility. Located in Korobosea, NCD, we provide advanced clinical services, specialist care, emergency treatment and support for patients from across the country.

Our partnership with UPNG

PMGH is the principal teaching hospital for the University of Papua New Guinea, School of Medicine and Health Sciences. We support undergraduate and postgraduate medical, nursing and health science training.

Services we provide

  • Emergency and trauma services
  • Medical and surgical specialties
  • Maternity and women’s health services
  • Paediatrics and neonatal care
  • Diagnostic imaging and pathology
  • Intensive care, high dependency and coronary care units

Our mission

To deliver high-quality, safe and effective healthcare for the people of Papua New Guinea, while supporting clinical education, training and research.

Our vision

Better health outcomes through excellence in clinical care, teaching and innovation.
