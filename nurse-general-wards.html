Our nursing teams provide compassionate, professional care for patients from across PNG.

Position: Registered Nurse – General Wards

Department: Nursing Services

Location: Port Moresby General Hospital, Korobosea

Employment type: Full time

About the role

The Registered Nurse – General Wards provides high-quality patient care in PMGH’s medical and surgical wards. The role includes direct clinical care, patient education, coordination of care and support for students and junior staff.

Key responsibilities

  • Deliver safe, evidence-based nursing care for inpatients.
  • Administer medications and treatments in accordance with hospital protocols.
  • Monitor patient progress and escalate concerns to senior clinicians.
  • Participate in ward rounds, handovers and multidisciplinary meetings.
  • Support education and supervision of students and new staff.

Essential requirements

  • Registered Nurse qualification recognised in Papua New Guinea.
  • Current registration with the Nursing Council of PNG (or eligibility to obtain).
  • Recent clinical experience in acute care settings.
  • Good communication skills and ability to work in a team.
  • Commitment to patient safety and continuous improvement.

Desirable

  • Experience in medical, surgical or high-dependency care.
  • Experience working in a teaching or referral hospital.
