Project & Planning · 2025

Strengthening specialist and critical care services

Port Moresby General Hospital continues to work with national and international partners to improve critical care, emergency services and intensive care capacity. Planned upgrades aim to enhance patient safety and modernise essential facilities.

Strengthening teaching & training

Education & training · 2025

PMGH supports clinical training for medical, nursing and health science students through its partnership with the University of Papua New Guinea’s School of Medicine & Health Sciences.

Improving patient and visitor facilities

Facilities · 2024

Upgrades to wards, waiting areas and outpatient spaces help improve comfort, safety and accessibility for patients and visitors.

Community blood donation initiatives

Community · 2024

Voluntary blood donors play a vital role in surgery, trauma care and emergency treatment. PMGH encourages eligible donors to participate in donation programs.

Masks & infection prevention

Visitors are encouraged to follow current public health guidance. Masks and hand hygiene may be recommended in sensitive areas to protect vulnerable patients.

If you are unwell with flu-like symptoms, please contact your local health facility or call ahead before visiting the hospital.
