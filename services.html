PMGH provides a wide range of essential, specialist and emergency clinical services, supporting patients from the National Capital District and referrals from across Papua New Guinea.

Our clinical services

Emergency Department

24/7 emergency care for serious illness, trauma and critical conditions.

Medical Services

General medicine, cardiology, nephrology, oncology, infectious diseases and more.

Surgical Services

General, orthopaedic, ENT, obstetrics, gynaecology and other surgical specialties.

Maternity & Obstetrics

Antenatal care, labour ward, delivery suites and postnatal support.

Paediatrics

Specialist care for infants, children and adolescents.

Diagnostic Imaging

X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan and other medical imaging services.

Pathology & Laboratory

Blood tests, microbiology, histopathology and diagnostic screening.

Outpatient Clinics

Specialist clinics including medical, surgical and chronic disease management.

Support & allied health services

  • Physiotherapy
  • Pharmacy services
  • Nutrition and dietetics
  • Social work and family support
  • Biomedical and technical services
