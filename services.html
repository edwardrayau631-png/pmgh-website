PMGH provides a wide range of services, from emergency and critical care to outpatient clinics, maternity services and diagnostic support. Some services require referral from a health centre, clinic or another hospital.

Emergency care

24-hour emergency care for serious illness, trauma and accidents.

Inpatient services

Medical and surgical wards caring for admitted patients who need ongoing treatment.

Outpatient clinics

Specialist and general clinics providing assessment, follow-up and review.

Maternity & women’s health

Antenatal clinics, labour ward, postnatal care and gynaecology services.

Paediatric services

Care for infants, children and adolescents through wards and clinics.

Diagnostic services

Radiology, imaging and pathology services to support diagnosis and treatment.

Support & counselling

Social work, Family Support Centre and other support for patients and families.

Education & training

Clinical teaching and training in partnership with UPNG.

For detailed information about a specific service, please speak with your referring clinician or contact the hospital switchboard.
