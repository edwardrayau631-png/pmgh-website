Port Moresby General Hospital provides a wide range of services, from emergency and intensive care to maternity, outpatient clinics and diagnostic support.

Emergency care

24/7 urgent care for serious illness and injury.

Inpatient services

Medical and surgical inpatient wards for admitted patients.

Maternity & child health

Antenatal, childbirth and postnatal services for mothers and infants.

Outpatient clinics

Specialist and general outpatient clinics for follow-up care.

Diagnostic services

Radiology, imaging and laboratory testing.

Support services

Social work, counselling and support for families and carers.
