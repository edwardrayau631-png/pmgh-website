Who we are

Port Moresby General Hospital (PMGH) is Papua New Guinea’s largest public hospital, located in Korobosea, Port Moresby. PMGH provides tertiary referral services and specialist care for patients from across the country.

As a major teaching hospital, PMGH plays a key role in training doctors, nurses and other health professionals in partnership with the University of Papua New Guinea’s School of Medicine & Health Sciences.

Our mission

To deliver safe, high-quality, compassionate healthcare for all people in Papua New Guinea and support education, training and research that strengthens the health system.

Our vision

To be a leading national referral and teaching hospital providing world-class care to every patient.

Our values

  • Respect and dignity for patients, families and staff
  • Excellence in clinical care and patient safety
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Continuous learning and improvement
  • Accountability and integrity
