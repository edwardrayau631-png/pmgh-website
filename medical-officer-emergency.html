Position: Medical Officer – Emergency Department

Department: Emergency Medicine

Location: PMGH, Korobosea

Type: Full time

About the role

The Medical Officer provides urgent and acute care for patients presenting to the PMGH Emergency Department. You will manage trauma, medical, surgical, paediatric and obstetric emergencies under supervision of senior specialists.

Key responsibilities

  • Assess, stabilise and treat emergency patients.
  • Order and interpret diagnostic investigations.
  • Perform emergency procedures (under supervision if required).
  • Maintain accurate clinical documentation.
  • Support junior medical officers and students.

Essential requirements

  • Medical degree recognised in PNG.
  • Registration with the PNG Medical Board (or eligibility to obtain).
  • Experience in emergency or acute care settings.
  • Ability to work in fast-paced environments.

Desirable

  • Experience in emergency medicine or critical care.
  • Training in trauma management or life support (ATLS, ACLS etc.).
