Working at PMGH

Be part of a dedicated multi-disciplinary team providing specialist care to patients from Port Moresby and across Papua New Guinea.

Why work with us?

  • PNG’s largest public and national referral hospital
  • Strong teaching partnerships with the University of Papua New Guinea
  • Opportunities for professional development and postgraduate training
  • Dynamic teams covering a full range of clinical specialties
  • Meaningful work improving the health of communities across PNG
