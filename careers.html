Work with us

Port Moresby General Hospital (PMGH) offers exceptional career opportunities for health professionals and support staff. As PNG’s national referral and teaching hospital, we are committed to delivering high-quality care and training the next generation of medical, nursing and allied health professionals.

Why work at PMGH?

  • Be part of PNG’s leading hospital for specialist and tertiary services
  • Work in a fast-growing, dynamic clinical environment
  • Opportunities for postgraduate training and professional development
  • Supported by partnerships with the University of Papua New Guinea
  • Meaningful work that impacts lives across Papua New Guinea

How to apply

Please send your application with the following documents:

  • Updated CV
  • Cover letter addressed to the PMGH Human Resources Manager
  • Certified copies of qualifications
  • Professional registrations (if applicable)
  • National ID or passport

Email applications to: hr@pmgh.gov.pg

For in-person submissions, please visit the PMGH HR Office, Administration Building.
