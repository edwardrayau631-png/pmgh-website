Working at PMGH

Join a dedicated workforce delivering specialist healthcare for patients from Port Moresby, Central Province and across Papua New Guinea.

Why work with us?

  • PMGH is PNG’s largest public hospital and national referral centre.
  • Strong teaching partnership with the University of Papua New Guinea.
  • Opportunities for postgraduate study and career progression.
  • A wide range of clinical specialties and multidisciplinary teams.
  • Meaningful, impactful work improving health outcomes across PNG.
