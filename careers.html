Working at PMGH

Be part of a dedicated team delivering specialist care for patients from Port Moresby, surrounding provinces and across Papua New Guinea.

Why work with us?

  • PNG’s largest public hospital and national referral centre
  • Strong teaching and training culture with the University of Papua New Guinea
  • Opportunities for postgraduate study and career progression
  • Multidisciplinary teams across a wide range of specialties
  • Meaningful work improving health outcomes for communities across PNG
